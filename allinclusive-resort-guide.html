What You Need to Know About Each Brand

The honest details — what each brand does exceptionally well, and who they're really built for.

Adults Only · Couples Only
Sandals Resorts

The gold standard for romantic all-inclusive travel. Sandals pioneered the couples-only all-inclusive concept and still sets the benchmark for romance, service, and included amenities.

Best for Couples
What makes Sandals special
  • Couples-only creates an atmosphere that's simply more peaceful and romantic
  • Overwater bungalows at select Caribbean properties — some of the most stunning accommodations in the world
  • Butler service at upper-tier suites — someone handles everything
  • Unlimited scuba diving included (PADI certified or resort dives)
  • Some of the best food in the all-inclusive space — many restaurants, genuinely good quality
  • Love Is All You Need® policy — same-sex couples equally welcome
Who should book Sandals
  • Honeymooners and anniversary couples who want everything to feel intentional
  • Anyone who wants maximum pampering and romance
  • Scuba divers — the diving inclusion is extraordinary value
  • Couples who want a "bucket list" room — overwater villa, over-the-water hammock, butler service
Top Properties
  • Sandals Royal Caribbean (Jamaica) — overwater villas
  • Sandals Grande St. Lucian — most dramatic setting
  • Sandals Emerald Bay (Bahamas) — remote luxury

Vanessa's take: If you're celebrating something — a honeymoon, anniversary, milestone birthday — Sandals is where I send couples first. The overwater villas in Jamaica are bucket-list material, and the scuba inclusion alone can save you hundreds per trip.

Adults Only · Upscale Luxury
Secrets Resorts & Spas

Where Sandals leans romantic, Secrets leans sophisticated. Part of the AMResorts family (now Apple Leisure Group), Secrets properties are sleek, modern, and built for adults who want luxury without the "couples-only" focus.

Best for Upscale Adults
What makes Secrets special
  • Stunning modern architecture — pools, suites, and public spaces that feel genuinely luxurious
  • Mexico locations (Cancún, Riviera Maya, Los Cabos) — often more accessible and more affordable than Caribbean
  • Preferred Club tier adds significant perks: upgraded rooms, private beach section, premium spirits, exclusive restaurant access
  • AMResorts loyalty program — great repeat value
  • Usually slightly more affordable than Sandals for comparable quality
Who should book Secrets
  • Adult couples who want luxury without the "honeymoon resort" atmosphere
  • Friend groups (all adults) wanting a high-end base in Mexico
  • Travelers who want Mexico's food/culture scene beyond the resort
  • Budget-conscious luxury travelers — Secrets often beats Sandals on price
Top Properties
  • Secrets Maroma Beach (Riviera Maya) — world-class beach
  • Secrets Akumal (Riviera Maya) — snorkeling right off the beach
  • Secrets Cap Cana (Dominican Republic) — largest marina in the Caribbean

Vanessa's take: Secrets is my go-to when adults want luxury at a slightly more approachable price point, or when they specifically want Mexico. Preferred Club is absolutely worth the upgrade — ask me about it when we talk.

Family-Friendly · All Ages
Beaches Resorts

Sandals' family-friendly sister brand is built from the ground up for multi-generational travel. With Sesame Street characters, water parks, and Butler service for families, Beaches delivers the full Sandals experience for everyone — kids very much included.

Best for Families
What makes Beaches special
  • Sesame Street characters roam the resort — young kids absolutely love it
  • Turks & Caicos property sits on Grace Bay Beach — consistently ranked the world's #1 beach
  • Massive water parks and multiple pools keep kids busy all day
  • Xbox Play Lounge and teen programs mean older kids aren't bored
  • Same quality restaurants and service as Sandals — adults aren't compromising
  • Butler service available for families — life-changing with young kids
Who should book Beaches
  • Families with kids 3–12 — the sweet spot for Sesame Street and water parks
  • Multi-generational trips — grandparents through grandkids all have something to do
  • Parents who want a resort that genuinely entertains kids so adults can relax
  • Anyone who wants to experience Grace Bay Beach — it truly lives up to the hype
Properties
  • Beaches Turks & Caicos — the flagship, world-class on every level
  • Beaches Negril (Jamaica) — more laid-back, great beach
  • Beaches Ocho Rios (Jamaica) — smaller, more intimate

Vanessa's take: Beaches Turks & Caicos is the best family all-inclusive in the world — I'll stand behind that. It costs more than most, but families who go once go back every year. I can help you find the best room category for your family's needs.

All Ages · Activity-Focused
Club Med

The original all-inclusive — Club Med invented the concept in 1950. Today it's built around activity, energy, and the famous G.O. (Gentil Organisateur) staff who do everything from teach trapeze to run the nightly shows. If you want energy and variety, this is your brand.

Best for Active Families
What makes Club Med special
  • The broadest activity offering of any all-inclusive — flying trapeze, archery, snorkeling, sailing, tennis, and dozens more included
  • G.O. staff are unlike anything else — genuinely energetic, involved, and fun
  • Worldwide locations including France, Japan, and the Maldives — not just Caribbean
  • Mini Club Med kids program is genuinely great — structured, fun, and supervised all day
  • Generally more affordable than Sandals or Beaches for comparable quality
Who should book Club Med
  • Active families who want to DO things, not just lounge
  • Travelers who want to learn something — sailing, trapeze, water sports
  • Social travelers who love meeting new people and joining group activities
  • Anyone considering an international all-inclusive (Europe, Asia)
Top Caribbean Properties
  • Club Med Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) — the flagship Caribbean resort
  • Club Med Buccaneer's Creek (Martinique) — adults-focused, French-Caribbean vibe
  • Club Med Turquoise (Turks & Caicos) — incredible water sports

Vanessa's take: Club Med gets underrated because people don't know it well. For active families or anyone who gets bored just lying by a pool, it's the best option out there. The flying trapeze alone is worth the trip.

Which Brand Is Right for You?

Not sure where to start? Here's the quick guide based on what matters most to you.

Choose Sandals if…
Sandals
  • You're celebrating a honeymoon or anniversary
  • You want romance above everything else
  • An overwater bungalow is on your bucket list
  • You or your partner want to scuba dive
  • You want butler service to handle every detail
Choose Secrets if…
Secrets
  • You want adult luxury without the romance theme
  • You prefer Mexico to the Caribbean
  • You want sleek, modern design over lush tropical
  • Budget matters and you want maximum value
  • You're going with a group of adult friends
Choose Beaches if…
Beaches
  • You're traveling with kids (especially ages 3–12)
  • You want the best beach in the Caribbean
  • You need grandparents and grandkids both happy
  • You want Sandals quality but with your whole family
  • You've been before and want to come back
Choose Club Med if…
Club Med
  • You get bored just lying on a beach
  • Your family loves sports and activities
  • You want more value than Sandals or Beaches
  • You're interested in a non-Caribbean destination
  • You love a social, high-energy atmosphere
